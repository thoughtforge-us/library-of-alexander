## Architecture

Qwen3-TTS is an open-source series of TTS models developed by the Qwen team at Alibaba.

## Key Design Patterns

1. **Open source** — Fully open source TTS models
2. **Multi-lingual** — Support for many languages
3. **High quality** — Competitive with commercial TTS
4. **Efficient inference** — Optimized for deployment
5. **Qwen ecosystem** — Integrates with Qwen models

## What We Can Learn

- Open source TTS enables customization
- Multi-lingual support is essential
- High quality competes with commercial options
- Efficient inference enables deployment
- Ecosystem integration improves usability
